Treatments

Dental Bonding

Bonding involves applying a tooth-colored composite resin to repair minor imperfections such as chips, cracks, or gaps in teeth.

Root Canal Therapy

This treatment is performed to save a tooth that is infected or severely decayed by removing the infected pulp and sealing the tooth to prevent further damage.

Tooth Extractions

Sometimes necessary for severely damaged or infected teeth, extractions involve the removal of a tooth from its socket in the jawbone.

Dental Implants

Implants are artificial tooth roots that are surgically placed into the jawbone to replace missing teeth. They provide a stable foundation for crowns, bridges, or dentures

Dental Crowns

Crowns are custom-made caps that are placed over damaged or weakened teeth to restore their strength, size, and appearance.

Dental Bridges

Bridges are used to replace one or more missing teeth by anchoring artificial teeth (pontics) to adjacent natural teeth or implants.

Orthodontic Treatments

Orthodontic treatments such as braces and clear aligners (e.g., Invisalign®) are used to straighten misaligned teeth and correct bite issues.

Teeth Whitening

Professional teeth whitening treatments help remove stains and discoloration, brightening the appearance of teeth and enhancing your smile.

Dental Veneers

Veneers are thin shells made of porcelain or composite resin that are bonded to the front surface of teeth to improve their appearance by correcting imperfections like chips, stains, or gaps.
